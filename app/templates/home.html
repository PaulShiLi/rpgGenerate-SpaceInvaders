But how does it work?

We threw some text at a robot...

We fed our machine a lot of text, now it spits out similar text!

But how does it really work?

We used Python, NLTK, Flask, GPT-3, and GPT-2 to make Nexus. Our text generation models are trained with Distil GPT-2 and datasets with over thousands of entries. The machine spends many hours processing the data, it learns to imitate what it was given.
