Nexus - the ultimate character-generating device

We had no imagination, so we made a robot instead.

When our creative juices run out, technology has our back. Nexus is here. Nexus is a tri-model engine powered by AI to generate character descriptions featuring personality traits, physical descriptions, and even backstories.

But how does it really work?

We used Python, NLTK, Flask, GPT-3, and GPT-2 to make Nexus. Our text generation models are trained with Distil GPT-2 and datasets with over thousands of entries. The machine spends many hours processing the data, it learns to imitate what it was given.
